FAQ

Questions owners usually ask.

Do you work with my existing vendors and crew/captain?

Yes. We can coordinate with your preferred vendors and serve as your on-the-ground point of contact.

How do approvals work?

You receive estimates for approval before work begins unless you authorize an emergency threshold in advance.

How often are inspections performed?

Most owners choose weekly checks, plus additional visits before/after major weather and ahead of arrivals.

Can you prep the boat before we arrive?

Absolutely. Turnkey prep is one of the biggest benefits for absentee owners.
